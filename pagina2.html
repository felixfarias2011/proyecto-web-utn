Grugru EP

Artist : DJ Deep, Traumer

Label : Tatum | TTM002

Styles : Minimal House

Fusing breakbeat and jazz samples into modern grooves is the idea of Dj Deep’s version, the track starts off with a heavy break beat that builds into a wild House journey to end into a more modern vision of this jam, as if a second track was mixed in. While Traumer delivers a 10mn beat driven trip where vocals and keyboards flirt along this haunting journey.
